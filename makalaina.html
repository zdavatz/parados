SETUP: Player 1, place both collectors.

DRAFT: Pick a disc to place on the board

SACRIFICE: Pick a disc (value = move range)

ColorP1P2

MAKA LAINA Rules

Goal

Collect discs on a 12x12 grid to score the most points. The game continues until all discs are collected or both players cannot move.

Shifting & Collection

  • Select a disc that is not in your Line of Sight (LOS).
  • Shift it 1 square so it enters your LOS.
  • Your collector moves to that square and collects the disc.
  • LOS extends in 8 directions, blocked by other discs or collectors.

Draft System

  • After collecting, 3 new discs are drawn from the bag.
  • The collecting player picks 1 and places it in their line of sight (so it can't be collected immediately).
  • The other player collects, then picks 1 of the remaining 2 and places it in their LOS.
  • The first player takes the last disc and places it in their LOS.
  • This repeats 8 times (24 discs total). The starting player alternates each cycle.
  • 54 discs total: 24 start on the board, 24 are drafted in, 6 stay hidden.

Special Mechanics

  • Number 1: Immediate extra turn.
  • Number 6: Future discs of that color are worthless for you.
  • Stuck? Sacrifice a collected primary disc: your score for that color decreases, but your collector can move up to that many squares freely.
